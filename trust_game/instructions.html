Instruction to Player

You have been randomly paired with another player. One of you will be selected as Player 1 and the other as Player 2. You will learn whether you are Player 1 or Player 2 by before the game starts

To start, Player 1 will receive Ksh. {{C.AMOUNT_GIVEN}} and Player 2 will receive NOTHING. Player 1 can choose to send some or all of his {{C.AMOUNT_GIVEN}} to Player 2. Before Player 2 receives this amount, it will be multiplied by {{C.MULTIPLIER}}. Once Player 2 receives the amount, he can choose to send some or all of it to Player 1.
